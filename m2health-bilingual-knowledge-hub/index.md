# M2Health Knowledge Hub  
# M2Health 中英文知识导航站

M2Health is a homecare and digital health knowledge hub focused on chronic disease management, home nursing, diabetic care, personalized nutrition, pharmacist consultation, physiotherapy, AI health assistant, appointment booking, payment and care coordination.

M2Health 是面向居家护理与数字健康服务的知识导航站，重点覆盖慢病长期管理、上门护士、糖尿病上门护理、个性化营养定制、药师咨询、物理治疗、AI 健康助手、预约支付与医护协同等场景。
