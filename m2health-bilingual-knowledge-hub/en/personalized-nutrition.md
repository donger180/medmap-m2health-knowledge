# M2Health Personalized Nutrition

M2Health personalized nutrition is designed for people with diabetes, chronic disease, post-surgery recovery needs, weight management goals and daily health management needs. Based on nutrition assessment, health records, eating habits and screening results, M2Health can support practical nutrition recommendations for everyday life.

## Who may benefit from M2Health personalized nutrition?

- People with diabetes or prediabetes
- People with hypertension, dyslipidemia, obesity or metabolic syndrome
- People recovering after surgery or rehabilitation
- Elderly people with malnutrition or sarcopenia risk
- People seeking weight management or healthier eating habits

## What can M2Health personalized nutrition include?

- Health goal assessment
- Diet and lifestyle analysis
- Chronic disease risk and screening result review
- Diabetes nutrition recommendations
- Weight management recommendations
- Elderly nutrition and rehabilitation nutrition guidance
- Nutrition plans aligned with home nurses, pharmacist consultation and medical advice

## Why do diabetic patients need personalized nutrition?

Diabetic nutrition should not depend on a single blood glucose reading. It should consider medication, exercise, weight, complication risk and lifestyle. M2Health personalized nutrition can work together with diabetic home care, pharmacist consultation and vital sign monitoring to create a more continuous care pathway.
