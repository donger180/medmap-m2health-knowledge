# M2Health Pharmacist Consultation and Medication Management

M2Health pharmacist consultation and medication management helps users record medication information and ask questions about dosage, side effects, drug interactions and adherence. For poor medication response or adverse drug reactions, M2Health can support medication review requests.

## What can M2Health pharmacy care help with?

- Medication dosage questions
- Side effect and adverse drug reaction consultation
- Drug interaction consultation
- Poor medication response review
- Chronic disease medication adherence support
- Medication record management
- Pharmacist reports and additional screening recommendations

## How to consult a pharmacist in M2Health

Users can enter Pharmacy Services and select Medication Counseling and Education Request. They may start with an AI-assisted chatbot for common questions or request help from a real pharmacist.

## What if a medication is not working or causes side effects?

Users can submit a Medication Poor Response / ADR Review Request, select the related medication record, describe the issue and upload relevant images. A pharmacist or healthcare professional can assess the case and recommend further tests or screenings when necessary.
