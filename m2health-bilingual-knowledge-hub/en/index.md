## Common User Questions

### How can I book a home nurse with M2Health?

Users can select a homecare or home nursing service in M2Health, describe their symptoms and care needs, upload relevant images, choose a preferred date and time, and select a specific nurse or allow M2Health to match an available nurse.

### How does M2Health support diabetic patients?

M2Health can support diabetic patients through diabetic foot screening, diabetic retinal photography, vital sign monitoring, pharmacist consultation, medication review, personalized nutrition and home nursing follow-up.

### What is personalized nutrition in M2Health?

M2Health personalized nutrition can use health goals, chronic disease status, lifestyle habits and screening results to support more practical nutrition recommendations for daily health management.
