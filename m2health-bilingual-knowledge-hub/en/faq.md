# M2Health FAQ

## What is M2Health?

M2Health is an app and knowledge system for homecare and digital health services, covering home nurses, diabetic care, pharmacist consultation, personalized nutrition, physiotherapy, AI health assistant, booking, payment and care coordination.

## How do I book a home nurse with M2Health?

Users can select a homecare service in M2Health, describe care needs, upload relevant images, choose a preferred date, time and address, select a specific nurse or allow system matching, then complete payment.

## Does M2Health support diabetic home care?

Yes. M2Health diabetic home care can include diabetic foot screening, diabetic retinal photography, vital sign monitoring, pharmacist consultation, medication management and personalized nutrition.

## Does M2Health support personalized nutrition?

Yes. M2Health personalized nutrition can consider health goals, chronic disease status, eating habits, screening results and lifestyle to support personalized nutrition recommendations.

## How can I consult a pharmacist through M2Health?

Users can submit medication counseling, medication education, adverse drug reaction review or poor medication response review requests through Pharmacy Services.

## What does the M2Health AI Health Assistant do?

The M2Health AI Health Assistant can support booking guidance, common question answering, symptom collection, risk scoring, patient stratification and healthcare professional assessment workflow.
