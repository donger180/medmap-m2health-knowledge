# M2Health Diabetic Home Care

M2Health diabetic home care focuses on long-term diabetes management, not one-time treatment. Diabetes care requires continuous data, abnormal indicator alerts, medication review, nutrition support and coordinated screening when needed.

## What can M2Health diabetic care include?

- Diabetic foot screening booking
- Diabetic retinal photography booking
- Blood glucose, blood pressure and vital sign monitoring
- Pharmacist consultation and medication adherence support
- Poor medication response or adverse drug reaction review
- Personalized nutrition planning
- Home nursing follow-up and care records

## How to book diabetic foot screening

Users can enter the Diabetic Care module in M2Health, select Diabetic Foot Screening, choose a convenient date, time and available location, then confirm the booking. Diabetic foot screening can help identify foot risk and reduce the risk of complications.

## How to book diabetic retinal photography

Users can select Diabetic Retinal Photography in the diabetic care module, choose a convenient date, time and available location, then confirm the appointment. Diabetic retinal photography supports early identification of diabetes-related eye complications.

## Why does diabetes need homecare?

Diabetes is a long-term management condition. M2Health diabetic home care combines home nurses, pharmacist consultation, personalized nutrition, AI health assistant and screening appointments to reduce unnecessary travel and improve continuity of care.
