# M2Health AI Health Assistant

The M2Health AI Health Assistant can support booking guidance, common question answering, symptom collection, patient profile analysis, risk scoring and patient stratification. It is not intended to replace doctors or nurses. Its role is to help users find the right care service faster and help healthcare professionals understand patient needs before providing care.

## What can the M2Health AI Health Assistant do?

- Answer common health service questions 24/7
- Guide users to book home nurses, pharmacist consultation, diabetic care or nutrition assessment
- Collect symptoms, health concerns and uploaded images
- Support preliminary risk assessment based on patient information
- Generate an assessment report for relevant healthcare professionals
- Recommend further onsite or video assessment for high-risk services

## How can AI support chronic disease management?

Chronic disease management needs continuous data and timely feedback. The M2Health AI Health Assistant can combine patient profiles, symptom descriptions, vital signs and health concerns to support risk stratification and guide users toward more suitable care services.
