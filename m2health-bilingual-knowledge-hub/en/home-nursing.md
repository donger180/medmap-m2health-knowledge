# M2Health Home Nursing and Homecare

M2Health home nursing is designed for patients and caregivers who need professional healthcare support at home. Through M2Health, users can book home nurses for primary nursing, specialized nursing, post-surgery care, wound dressing, medication assistance, vital sign monitoring and chronic disease follow-up.

## Who may need M2Health home nursing?

- Post-surgery patients
- Chronic disease patients, including diabetes, hypertension and cardiovascular conditions
- Elderly people or people with limited mobility
- Patients requiring wound care, catheter care, injection support or medication assistance
- Patients who need regular vital sign monitoring and nursing follow-up

## How to book a home nurse with M2Health

1. Open the M2Health App.
2. Select Home Health Care or Homecare.
3. Choose Primary Nursing Services or Specialized Nursing Services.
4. Enter symptoms, care needs, service address and relevant images.
5. Select the required nursing procedure, date and time.
6. Choose a specific nurse or allow M2Health to match an available nurse.
7. Review the cost and complete payment.

## Examples of primary nursing services

- Wound dressing
- Medication assistance
- Vital sign monitoring
- Chronic disease nursing follow-up
- Home health assessment

## Examples of specialized nursing services

- Post-surgery care
- IV therapy support
- Catheter management
- Specialized nursing assessment
- Home nursing services requiring trained professionals
