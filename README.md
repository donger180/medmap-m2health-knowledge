# M2Health

> Connected Home Healthcare Platform powered by AI

**Bringing Professional Healthcare to Every Home**

M2Health is an intelligent digital healthcare platform developed by **MedMap Pte Ltd**, connecting patients, caregivers, nurses, pharmacists, doctors, and allied healthcare professionals through one integrated mobile platform.

Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible, continuous, and personalized by combining home healthcare services, AI-powered assistance, telehealth, and chronic disease management.

# Vision

Healthcare should not stop after a clinic visit.

M2Health transforms traditional episodic care into **continuous healthcare management**, enabling patients to receive professional medical support wherever they are.

Our platform supports:

- Home Healthcare
- Chronic Disease Management
- Digital Pharmacy
- Preventive Care
- Remote Healthcare
- AI-assisted Patient Assessment

# AI Healthcare Assistant

M2Health includes an AI-powered virtual healthcare assistant available 24/7.

The AI Assistant helps users:

- Navigate healthcare services
- Book appointments
- Understand symptoms
- Assess health risks
- Receive medication guidance
- Find appropriate healthcare providers

The AI also assists healthcare professionals by performing preliminary patient assessment before appointments.

# Intelligent Patient Assessment

Before a home visit or consultation, M2Health can perform preliminary assessment using:

- Patient profile
- Medical history
- Symptoms
- Uploaded photos
- Risk scoring
- AI-assisted triage

High-risk patients may be referred for video consultation or additional assessment before service delivery.
